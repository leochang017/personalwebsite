NapkinNotes

AI-Powered EdTech Platform

Co-Founder & Lead Developer Active Development Aug 2025 - Present

Project Overview

NapkinNotes is an innovative EdTech startup that provides students with an AI-powered platform to share and transform class notes into concise, course-specific study resources. The platform also features a peer-to-peer marketplace where students can buy and sell used textbooks and school supplies.

Key Features

  • AI-powered note summarization and transformation
  • Peer-to-peer marketplace for textbooks and supplies
  • Course-specific study resource generation
  • Student-friendly interface and experience
  • Collaborative note sharing platform

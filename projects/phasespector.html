Project Overview

Phase Spector is a top-down wave-based arcade shooter built in Godot 4.6 featuring a unique time-rewind mechanic, available for 500+ PDS students to play. Players hold the rewind button to travel backward through their recent movement, then unleash area-of-effect attacks along the entire rewind path. The mechanic doubles as both a defensive dodge and an offensive weapon, creating high-risk, high-reward gameplay.

The game features escalating difficulty across endless waves, three distinct enemy types with unique behaviors, a chain-kill score multiplier system, projectile deflection, and persistent high score leaderboards.
